Gerund VS Infinitive

guide
22 oct, 2022

What is gerund?

A gerund is a verb form that ends in ing:

  • working
  • reading
  • running
  • traveling
  • drawing
  • learning
  • playing
  • watching
  • sleeping

What is infinitive?

An infinitive is the base form of a verb preceded by to:

  • to work
  • to read
  • to run
  • to travel
  • to draw
  • to learn
  • to play
  • to watch
  • to sleep

Gerund usage

1. After prepositions

I'm good at eating cakes

My mom is scared of flying

I have an interest in learning English

Van gogh is famous for using unique colors

My dog is keen on sleeping

2. After some verbs

There are common verbs followed only by a gerund: admit, enjoy, avoid, deny, dislike, feel like, keep, mind, miss, practise, recommend, spend time, stop, suggest.

They admitted changing the schedule

I enjoy learning English

I avoid walking alone at night

She deny knowing anything about her ex-boyfriend

I dislike watching TV news

I don't feel like dancing

I keep hoping everything will be okay.

Would you mind helping me?
